Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I get a response?
We typically reply within 2–4 hours during business days (Mon–Sat, 9am–7pm IST). For urgent requests, mention it in your message subject.
How do you match me with a freelancer?
Once you submit your project details, our team reviews your requirements and manually selects the best-fit freelancer from our vetted network within 24 hours.
Do you work with international clients?
Absolutely. We serve clients across the US, UK, Europe, Australia, and Southeast Asia. All communication is in English and payments are supported globally.
What if I'm not satisfied with the work?
We offer unlimited revisions within the project scope and a satisfaction guarantee. Your success is our priority — we don't close a project until you're happy.
Can I apply to become a Bridge freelancer?
Yes! Select "Freelancer Application" in the contact form and send us your portfolio and areas of expertise. We review applications every two weeks.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept bank transfers, UPI, PayPal, and major credit/debit cards. A 50% deposit secures your project start date, with the remaining 50% on delivery.
